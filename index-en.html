DIN&CO

History and business

Din&Co freight forwarding company is, as of this year, 20 years in the business of providing services in intermediation of export and import.

There is a wide variety of services we offer. Each of our clients has a guaranteed full attention and time of our skilled workers, in fast and efficient completion of customs intermediation – spedition.

We represent clients in export-import of all kinds of goods, in all forms of transport – road, railway, ship and aerotransport.
