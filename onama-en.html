ABOUT US

Din&Co
Forwarding administration

„Din&Co“ freight forwarder, is a small family company founded in 1995., while the experience in freight forwarding exists from 1989. We are in the mediation of exporting and importing goods for our clients and we do every activity needed for the job.
With our capacity, expertise and agile staff, we are capable to cover the entire territory of Vojvodina and Belgrade, and offer our services to everyone who is into international trade and transport.
